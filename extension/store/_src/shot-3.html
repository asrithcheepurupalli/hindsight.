Provably local.

Your face is yours. rearview is built so it cannot leave your device.

No network permission

The extension cannot make network requests. Not "does not", cannot. The permission is never requested. Watch your network tab stay silent.

Five-second memory

Frames live in memory only, just long enough to play back the delay, then they are gone. Nothing is ever written to disk.

Nothing collected

No account, no analytics, no cookies, no servers. Your only data is your settings, stored in your own browser.

Open source, so you do not have to take our word for it.
