Rearview

See yourself the way they just saw you.

On a video call, the moment you glance at your self-view your eyes have already moved — so you only ever see your checking-the-mirror face, never your real mid-conversation face.

Rearview plays your camera back on a short delay. When you glance over, you're watching live, moving video of yourself from a few seconds ago — eyes still on the screen, still talking — exactly how the other person saw you. No freezing, no screenshots.

Everything stays on your device. Nothing is recorded or uploaded.

How far back in time your mirror looks. 0s = a normal live mirror.

Off = unmirrored, how others actually see you. On = like a bathroom mirror.

Tip: pop out the floating window and park it near your webcam, then get on your call. Keep this tab visible (or popped out) so the delayed feed keeps running.

Runs entirely in your browser · no account · no servers · open source
